BRAIN AI

It's a chatbot created by us to deliver answers for domain-specific FAQs in an easy-to-understand manner. Designed for accuracy and efficiency, it dynamically retrieves structured data and recognizes relationships to provide insightful responses. With a focus on both privacy and performance, it offers flexibility through offline and online modes. Whether running locally or on the cloud, it ensures seamless interaction while adapting to user needs.

Brain.AI Chatbot provides accurate and context-aware responses for domain-specific FAQs by leveraging structured data retrieval and relationship recognition.

It is available in two versions: an offline model and an online model. The offline version runs locally, ensuring privacy, security, and customization without internet dependency. It provides full control over various parameters and utilizes a vast dataset with over 7 billion data points. The online version, on the other hand, is cloud-based, offering faster performance and scalability.

The chatbot is powered by Oobabooga, which enhances LLM performance. It integrates advanced models such as Yarn-Mistral-7B, Llama-3-Nephilim, and DeepSeek Rt-Distill-Qwen-7B. Additionally, it employs cutting-edge NLP technologies like T5 for text transformation, Sileron and Coqui for text-to-speech, and Whisper-STT for speech recognition.
